# Atomic and Nuclear Physics
### Class 12 – Tamil Nadu State Board
---

## Core Concept
This chapter explains the **structure of atoms and nuclei**, and the principles governing them.

* Foundation of modern physics
* Explains matter at microscopic level
* Basis for nuclear energy and applications

---

## 01. Atomic Models
Development of atomic structure theories.

* **:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} Model:** Atom as a positive sphere with embedded electrons.
* **:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1} Model:** Nuclear model with dense central nucleus.
* **:contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2} Model:** Electrons revolve in fixed energy levels.

---

## 02. Photoelectric Effect
Emission of electrons when light falls on metal surface.

* Explained by **:contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}**
* Introduces concept of **photons**
* Depends on frequency, not intensity

---

## 03. Bohr’s Theory
Explains hydrogen spectrum.

* Electrons move in quantized orbits
* Energy levels are discrete
* Radiation emitted during transitions

:contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

---

## 04. Dual Nature of Matter
Matter shows both wave and particle nature.

* Proposed by **:contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}**
* Applies to electrons and microscopic particles

:contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

---

## 05. Nuclear Structure
Study of nucleus and its properties.

* Contains protons and neutrons
* Strong nuclear force binds nucleons
* Nuclear size is very small compared to atom

---

## 06. Radioactivity
Spontaneous emission of radiation.

* Discovered by **:contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}**
* Types:
  - Alpha (α)
  - Beta (β)
  - Gamma (γ)

---

## 07. Half-Life
Time taken for half of the radioactive substance to decay.


::contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}


* Important for nuclear stability
* Used in carbon dating and medicine

---

## 08. Nuclear Fission & Fusion

### Nuclear Fission
* Splitting of heavy nucleus into smaller nuclei
* Used in nuclear reactors

### Nuclear Fusion
* Combining light nuclei to form heavy nucleus
* Source of energy in the Sun

## Applications
Real-world importance of atomic and nuclear physics:

* Nuclear energy production
* Medical imaging and cancer therapy
* Particle accelerators
* Space research
