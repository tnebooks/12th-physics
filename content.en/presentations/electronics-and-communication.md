# Electronics and Communication
### Class 12 – Tamil Nadu State Board
---

## Core Concept
This chapter explains how **electronic devices control current flow** and how **information is transmitted over distances**.

* Foundation of modern electronics
* Basis for communication systems
* Widely used in digital technology

---

## 01. Semiconductors
Materials with conductivity between conductors and insulators.

* Examples: Silicon, Germanium  
* Conductivity increases with temperature  
* Used in electronic devices  

---

## 02. Types of Semiconductors

### Intrinsic Semiconductor
* Pure semiconductor  
* Equal number of electrons and holes  

### Extrinsic Semiconductor
* Doped semiconductor  

#### n-type
* Excess electrons  

#### p-type
* Excess holes  

---

## 03. PN Junction Diode
Basic semiconductor device.

* Formed by joining p-type and n-type materials  
* Allows current in one direction  

---

## 04. Diode as Rectifier

* Converts AC into DC  

### Half-Wave Rectifier
* Uses one diode  

### Full-Wave Rectifier
* Uses two or four diodes  

---

## 05. Transistor
Used as amplifier and switch.

* Types:
  - NPN
  - PNP  
* Terminals:
  - Emitter  
  - Base  
  - Collector  

---

## 06. Logic Gates
Basic building blocks of digital electronics.

### AND Gate
* Output is high only if both inputs are high  

### OR Gate
* Output is high if any one input is high  

### NOT Gate
* Inverts the input  

---

## 07. Communication System
Transfer of information from sender to receiver.

* Components:
  - Transmitter  
  - Channel  
  - Receiver  

---

## 08. Modulation
Process of superimposing signal on carrier wave.

* Types:
  - Amplitude Modulation (AM)  
  - Frequency Modulation (FM)  

---

## Applications
Importance in daily life:

* Mobile communication  
* Television and radio  
* Computers and digital systems  
* Internet and networking
