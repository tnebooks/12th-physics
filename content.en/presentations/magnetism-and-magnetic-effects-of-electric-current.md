# Magnetism and Magnetic Effects of Electric Current
### Class 12 – Tamil Nadu State Board
---

## Core Concept
This chapter deals with **magnetic fields produced by electric current** and their effects on charges and conductors.

* Links electricity and magnetism
* Basis for motors and generators
* Important for electromagnetic devices

---

## Magnetic Field
Region around a magnet or current-carrying conductor where magnetic force is experienced.

* Represented by magnetic field lines  
* Unit: Tesla (T)  

---

## Magnetic Field due to Current
A current-carrying conductor produces a magnetic field.

* Direction given by right-hand thumb rule  

---

## Biot–Savart Law
Magnetic field due to a small current element.

* Describes field variation with distance and current  

---

## Ampere’s Circuital Law

* Relates magnetic field with current

## Magnetic Field in Special Cases

### Straight Conductor
* Field decreases with distance  

### Circular Loop
* Field at center increases with turns  

### Solenoid
* Produces uniform magnetic field
