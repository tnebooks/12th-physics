## Core Concept
This chapter explains the **behavior of light using rays**, including reflection and refraction.

* Basis for optical instruments  
* Explains image formation  
* Important for vision and lenses  

---

## 01. Reflection of Light

* Light bounces back from a surface  
* Laws of reflection:
  - Angle of incidence = Angle of reflection  
  - Incident ray, reflected ray, and normal lie in same plane
